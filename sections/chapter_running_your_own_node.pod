=head0 Running Your Own Node

You can do a lot with Dogecoin if you let someone else manage your wallet, but
taking control of your own coins gives you a lot of power and security. While
this may initially seem daunting, it can be easier than you think after you
learn a few things.

In this chapter, we'll talk about interesting things you can do when you run
your own node.
